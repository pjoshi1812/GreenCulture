About Us


GREEN CULTURE was founded in 1978 and has earned its reputation as one of the leading firms working in the field of plant nurseries and providing the information about vegetable,flowers,fruits For several years now. Green Culture has become synonymous to greenery. We pride ourselves on superior quality, reliability, professionalism, and cost effectiveness in our every product and service We specialize in the design and maintenance of plants in the diverse settings of the workplace. With our well trained and experienced personnel, we are ideally suitable to the major business areas of the metropolis of Pune. We have our own trial grounds, nurseries and large green/shade houses near Alibag GREEN CULTURE NURSERIES E-Store Our retail and wholesale garden centre in Mumbai is located in Chembur Plants are brought here mainly from our propogation nursery situated near Alibag Apart from sale of plants we also deal in garden accessories like ceramic and plastic pots, potting mixes, fertilizers, etc., making our garden center a 'one stop shop for all plant enthusiasts
